What is an Animal?

Animals are multicellular eukaryotes whose cells are bound together by collagen. They dominate human conceptions of life on Earth because of their size, diversity, abundance, and mobility. The presence of muscles and mobility is one of the primary characteristics of the animal kingdom.

Animals on Land

Animals that live on land are also called Terrestrial animals, as compared with aquatic animals, which live predominantly or entirely in the water, or amphibians, which rely on a combination of aquatic and terrestrial habitats.
